01 — Event Operations

Lead capture & event execution

What I ran

End-to-end event operations for a national trade-show calendar — pre-show planning, on-site lead capture, booth presence, and structured follow-up. Captured leads fed straight back into the CRM so they entered the pipeline cleanly instead of sitting on a badge scanner.

What I designed

The visual system behind each show — booth graphics, printed sales collateral, and webinar content — kept consistent across events so the brand looked the same whether a prospect met us in person or online.
