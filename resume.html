Sales Operations Manager  |  M.S. Business Analytics

I run commercial operations at EndyMed, an energy-based aesthetics device manufacturer. Day to day, that means owning the systems a small commercial team relies on — the CRM, how deals move from closed to installed, how service and end-of-life cases get handled, and how our marketing and event presence turns into real pipeline.

I like the operations side because it rewards clear thinking. A good process isn't just one that runs; it's one you can measure, question, and improve. That's the same instinct that pulled me into analytics — I wanted to move past “this feels like it's working” to actually knowing where the time, cost, and value are going.

I'm finishing an M.S. in Business Analytics while working full time, and I use what I learn in the classroom on the work in front of me — segmenting the install base, pressure-testing a process, or building a tool that saves the team a manual step. I'm looking for a commercial or sales operations role where building the operating system is the job, not a side project.
