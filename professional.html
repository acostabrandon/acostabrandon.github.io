Project 02

Client Onboarding System

One repeatable journey from signed deal to a certified, supported customer.

Automated Training Scheduling Digital Installation Verification Standardized Communications Cross-Functional Visibility
Problem

The customer journey became fragmented after the deal was signed — fulfillment, financing, installation, training, and marketing handoffs were disconnected.

Action

I built a client onboarding board, triggered at closed-won, that runs every customer through one standardized path — device, funding, shipping, installation, and training dates all tracked in a single shared view. It's the operational hub the whole handoff flows through.

One standardized path. Every customer moves through the same seven stages:

  1. Order Confirmed

  2. Shipment & Delivery

  3. Installation

  4. Schedule Training

  5. Training Confirmation

  6. Training Completed / Certification

  7. 30-Day Check-In

Action

Standardized customer communications

Every touchpoint uses a standardized template and resource set, delivered automatically during onboarding.

Required-Field Intake Automated Agreement Creation Approval Workflow Error Reduction
Problem

Action

1
PQ Request Form
A rep submits the required-field intake — sales rep, customer, and system configuration.
2
Power Automate
Submission triggers the flow — no manual kickoff.
3
Office Scripts

4
Template Updates
Terms, warranty, and pricing are populated into the agreement template.
5
Approval Email
A completed approval email is generated for internal review.
6
Spreadsheet Copy
A spreadsheet copy of the Purchase Agreement is auto-attached.
7
Client Signature
The finished agreement is ready to send for signature.

Project 05

SOP Development

Turning tribal knowledge into scalable, documented systems.

Internal Knowledge Library Sales Enablement Training Resources Standardized Service Workflows
Problem

Too many processes relied on tribal knowledge — inconsistent, hard to scale, and dependent on who happened to be handling them.

Action

I organized operating documentation and enablement into four scalable systems:

Internal Operations

SOPs, agreements, and protocols in one governed library.

Sales Enablement

Presentations, studies, and field resources reps can rely on.

Clinical Training

Prep materials, schedules, and certificates, standardized.

Service & Support

Documented, repeatable procedures for post-sale issues.

Deep dive — Replacement Tip Request. I replaced inconsistent, case-by-case replacement decisions with one repeatable, documented SOP.

Old workflow

  1. Customer reports a broken tip
  2. A single replacement is shipped, case by case
  3. Extra shipping cost and limited oversight
  4. Repeat-request risk

New workflow

  1. Customer submits a structured claim
  2. Issue reviewed against a documented policy
  3. Store credit issued toward the next order when appropriate
  4. Repeat / high-value issues escalated

Clear ownership

Every workflow has a defined owner and trigger.

Takeaway

Operations that once depended on individuals now run as documented, auditable systems that scale.
