01 — Research

Emerging Tech in OSCM — Research Pipeline

Turning 75K+ scattered records into a clean corpus for a systematic review.

As a research assistant at LMU, I owned the data and analysis behind a faculty systematic review of emerging technologies across operations and supply chain management. I built and refined an enrichment pipeline over a 75K+ article dataset from 50+ approved journals — cutting missing abstracts from roughly 80% to under 5% using OpenAlex, Crossref, and DOI APIs — then developed classification, topic-modeling, and visualization workflows in Python to surface the technology themes shaping the field.

Python pandas spaCy · gensim · NLTK OpenAlex / Crossref APIs Topic modeling Data enrichment
