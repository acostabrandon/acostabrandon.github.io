Analytics & Academic

M.S. Business Analytics projects

Six projects from my M.S. in Business Analytics, each built on a public dataset. Every deck is embedded below with a method-and-outcome summary; use the “Open full deck” link beneath any embed to view it directly, especially on mobile.

01 — Operations · Queuing Theory

Starbucks Queuing Model Analysis

Modeled customer flow through a Starbucks service line as a queuing system to test how arrival and service rates drive wait times and utilization. Used the model to identify where added capacity meaningfully reduces waiting versus where it barely moves the needle, translating queuing math into concrete staffing implications.

02 — Customer Analytics · RFM · CLV · Survival

Global Superstore Customer Lifetime Value

Segmented the Global Superstore customer base with RFM, estimated customer lifetime value, and applied survival analysis to model retention and churn timing. The combined view distinguished durable, high-value customers from those at risk, pointing to where retention effort earns the most return.

03 — Geographic Visualization

Regional Film-Genre Revenue Trends

Analyzed how film revenue by genre varies across regions, using geographic visualization to map where particular genres over- and under-perform. The maps turned a large, flat revenue table into a clear read on regional taste and distribution opportunity.

04 — NLP · Topic Modeling

Disneyland Review Topic Modeling

Applied LDA and NMF topic modeling to more than 42,000 Disneyland guest reviews to surface the themes driving satisfaction and complaints. Translated the recurring topics — wait times, value, and experience quality among them — into concrete guest-experience recommendations.

05 — Applied LLM · RAG · Streamlit

RAG Job-Fit Analyzer

Built a Streamlit tool that uses retrieval-augmented generation to compare a resume against a job description and surface the skill gaps between them. The app returns a structured fit assessment, showing where a candidate matches and what to strengthen before applying.
